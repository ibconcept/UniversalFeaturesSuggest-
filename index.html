How is UFS different from a Play Store review? expand_more

Play Store reviews are often emotional complaints about bugs. UFS is specifically for structured feature requests and UI/UX improvements that allow for community upvoting and direct bounty rewards.

Can companies pay users for their ideas? expand_more

Yes. UFS features a "Feature Bounty" system where companies post specific challenges or "claim" existing popular suggestions by paying the original architect in cash, credit, or reputation badges.
